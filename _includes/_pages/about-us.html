About us

We are an interdisciplinary team consisting of employees from the start-up re:edu and the Institute for Geoinformatics at the University of Münster. The idea for the senseBox originated in the ‘GI@School’ student and research laboratory, which aims to teach the basics of computer science and data skills in an application-oriented manner through the collection and evaluation of environmental data. The senseBox and its various kits were developed in collaboration as part of the senseBox:pro research project. Even after the end of the project, we continue to work together on various research projects to further develop the senseBox.
