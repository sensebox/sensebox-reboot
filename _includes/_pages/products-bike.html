The senseBox:bike

The senseBox:bike is an innovative device that transforms your bicycle into a mobile environmental sensor with advanced AI capabilities. It allows cyclists to collect valuable data about their surroundings while riding, contributing to urban environmental monitoring and citizen science projects. With its advanced sensors, real-time analysis capabilities, and on-device machine learning, the senseBox:bike empowers you to become an active participant in understanding and improving your local environment while enhancing your cycling safety.
